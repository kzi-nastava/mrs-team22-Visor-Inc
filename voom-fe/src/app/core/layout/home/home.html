How it Works?

Choose Your Pickup & Drop-off

Set where you’re starting and where you’re going. Use the map or search by address to quickly select your pickup and destination. You’ll see the estimated route and distance instantly.

Add Stops Along the Way

Need to stop somewhere in between? Add additional waypoints to your route — whether it’s picking someone up, making a quick stop, or changing plans mid-ride.

Get Matched With a Driver

Once your route is ready, nearby drivers are matched based on availability. You can track the driver in real time and see exactly how the ride progresses on the map.

Pay After the Ride

No upfront payment. Once you reach your final destination, you pay directly in the car. Simple, transparent, and hassle-free — you only pay for the ride you took.
